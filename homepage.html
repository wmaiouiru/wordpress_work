CLASS DESCRIPTIONS

Stretch, Tone & Relax

With a deep focus on alignment and the breath, we move you thoughtfully through poses that strengthen the muscles, stretch the body, and calm the mind. Through creative sequencing and tailored techniques, you’ll leave feeling stronger, more energized, looser, and lighter.

Relax & Restore

Melt away your stress with the R&R method. Through the use of essential oils, sound therapy, and a combination of deep breathing, stretching postures, and a deep relaxation meditation, you will enter into total relaxation and leave feeling peaceful, calm, and grounded. This experience uses custom mats developed by airweave, the leader in bedding technology, that support and cushion the body. This class is great for all looking to relax and restore, especially those who suffer from anxiety and insomnia.

ABOUT

Atthena first discovered yoga when she was ploughing through grueling hours as an investment banker on Wall Street.  Long hours sitting at a desk and the immense stress that came with the job caused her to gain a substantial amount of weight and an inability to shut off her racing mind.  She found that yoga not only provided the movement and stretching that countered the sedentary lifestyle, but also the stress relief that no other activity did.  What had started as a mere workout and hobby, turned into a passion and now life’s purpose.  Atthena is excited to share with you her love and joy for yoga and hope you fall in love with it and realize it’s incredible benefits as she has.
